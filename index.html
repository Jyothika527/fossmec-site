About FOSSMEC

FOSSMEC stands for the Free and Open Source Cell at Govt. Model Engineering College, Kochi (MEC). It's a community dedicated to promoting and fostering the Free and Open Source Software (FOSS) ecosystem, particularly within the college and beyond. They aim to cultivate a collaborative culture, encourage open-source contributions, and provide a platform for enthusiasts to learn and engage with open-source projects. FOSSMEC's primary goal is to promote and strengthen the FOSS ecosystem among MEC students and the wider community. They organize events, workshops, and collaborations to engage with open-source software and technologies. FOSSMEC serves as a hub for FOSS enthusiasts, providing a space for learning, sharing knowledge, and contributing to various open-source projects. They actively collaborate with other organizations, like IEEE MEC SB and Polygon Guild Kochi to organize events and initiatives.
